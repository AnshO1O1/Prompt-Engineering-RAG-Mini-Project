Cancellation Policy

Customers may request to cancel their order within **24 hours of placing the order**, provided the order has **not yet been shipped**. Orders cancelled within this time frame will not incur any cancellation charges.

Once an order has been shipped or dispatched, it cannot be cancelled. In such cases, customers are requested to accept the delivery and may initiate a **return request after delivery**, in accordance with the companyâ€™s **Return and Refund Policy**.

Approved Cancellations

If a cancellation request is approved: 
* Any prepaid amount paid for the order will be refunded as per the Refund Policy.
* Refunds will be processed using the **original payment method.
* The refund processing timeline will remain the same as that of standard refunds.

Important Notes:

* Cancellation requests must be submitted through the designated customer support channels.
* The company **reserves the right to approve or reject** cancellation requests that do not comply with the above conditions.
* Orders placed during sale events, promotions, or special offers may be subject to **additional cancellation restrictions**, if applicable.

For further assistance, customers are encouraged to contact our customer support team.
