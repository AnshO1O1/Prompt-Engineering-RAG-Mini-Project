Refund Policy

Customers are eligible to request a refund within 7 calendar days from the date of delivery of the product.

Eligibility for Refund:

To qualify for a refund, the following conditions must be met:
- The product must be **unused, unworn, unwashed, and in original condition, with all tags, labels, and packaging intact.
- A valid proof of purchase (order confirmation, invoice, or receipt) must be provided.
- The refund request must be initiated within 7 days** of receiving the product.

 Refund Process

- Once a return request is submitted, the product must be shipped back as per the instructions provided by customer support.
- Refunds are initiated only after the returned product is received, inspected, and approved by our quality control team.
- If the product does not meet the eligibility criteria, the refund request may be rejected, and the product will be returned to the customer.

- Approved refunds are processed using the original payment method used at checkout.

Additional Information

- Shipping or handling charges (if any) are non-refundable, unless the return is due to a damaged, defective, or incorrect item.
- Products purchased during sales, discounts, or promotional offers may be subject to different refund conditions.
- The company reserves the right to modify or deny refund requests that do not comply with this policy.
