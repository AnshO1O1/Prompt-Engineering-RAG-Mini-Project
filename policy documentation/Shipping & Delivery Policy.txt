

Order Processing
All orders are processed within 1 to 2 business days after successful order confirmation. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will be processed on the next business day.

Delivery Timelines:
- Domestic deliveries typically take 5 to 10 business days from the date of dispatch.
- Delivery timelines may vary based on the shipping destination, courier partner, and service availability in the area.

Possible Delays:
While we strive to deliver orders within the estimated time frame, delivery may occasionally be delayed due to factors beyond our control, including but not limited to:
- Weather conditions
- Logistics or courier delays
- Public holidays or regional restrictions
- Unforeseen operational issues.
Customers will be notified in case of significant delays whenever possible.

Shipping Coverage:
- At present, we only support domestic shipping.
- International shipping is not available at this time.

Additional Information
- Delivery timelines are estimates and should not be considered guaranteed.
- The company is not responsible for delays caused by incorrect or incomplete shipping details provided by the customer.
- For any delivery-related queries, customers are encouraged to contact our customer support team.
